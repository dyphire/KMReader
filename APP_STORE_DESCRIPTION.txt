KMReader - Full-Featured iOS & macOS Client for Komga

A comprehensive, native iOS and macOS client for Komga media servers. Manage and enjoy your comics, mangas, BDs, and magazines collection on the go.

FEATURES

• Complete Library Management: Browse Series, Books, Collections, and Read Lists with advanced search, filtering, and sorting
• Multiple Reading Modes: LTR, RTL, Vertical, and Webtoon (iOS only) with customizable settings
• Dual Page Mode: Enjoy two-page spreads in landscape orientation with optional cover skipping
• Auto-Sync Progress: Automatic reading progress synchronization across all devices
• Incognito Mode: Read without updating your progress
• Dashboard: Keep Reading, On Deck, Recently Added Books, Recently Added Series, and Recently Updated Series sections
• Reading History: Complete reading history with infinite scroll and quick resume
• Collections & Read Lists: Organize your library and create custom reading lists
• Server Management: View server information, metrics, and manage libraries (admin features)
• Smart Caching: Intelligent two-tier caching system (512MB-8GB configurable) for smooth, offline-capable experience
• Customizable Experience: Themes, layout modes, reader settings, page layouts, and more
• Save Pages: Export favorite pages to Photos or Files in multiple formats (JPEG, PNG, HEIF, WebP)
• macOS Support: Native macOS app with dedicated reader window for enhanced desktop reading experience

Connect to your Komga server and take full control of your digital library today.
