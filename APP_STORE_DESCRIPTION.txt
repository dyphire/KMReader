KMReader - Full-Featured iOS Client for Komga

A comprehensive, native iOS client for Komga media servers. Manage and enjoy your comics, mangas, BDs, and magazines collection on the go.

FEATURES

• Complete Library Management: Browse Series, Books, Collections, and Read Lists with advanced search, filtering, and sorting
• Multiple Reading Modes: LTR, RTL, Vertical, and Webtoon with customizable settings
• Auto-Sync Progress: Automatic reading progress synchronization across all devices
• Dashboard: Keep Reading, On Deck, and Recently Added sections for quick access
• Collections & Read Lists: Organize your library and create custom reading lists
• Server Management: View server information, metrics, and manage libraries (admin features)
• Smart Caching: Intelligent two-tier caching system for smooth, offline-capable experience
• Customizable Experience: Themes, layout modes, reader settings, and more

Connect to your Komga server and take full control of your digital library today.
